Server Rules

Short, clear rules to keep the server fun and fair.

  • No griefing or stealing other players' items or builds.
  • No hacking, x-ray, duping, or any client-side cheats.
  • No toxicity, racist language, or harassment.
  • Do not abuse exploits or lag machines (keep tick load reasonable).
  • Respect staff decisions — impersonation of staff is forbidden.
  • No advertising other servers or unsolicited links.
  • Use PvP only where allowed or when both players consent.

Breaking the rules may result in warnings, temporary bans, or permanent bans depending on severity.
